(b. 1997) is a photographer and visual artist based in Italy. After graduating at Ca’ Foscari University of Venice he shifted his focus towards photography. Currently undertaking the MA in Photography at ISIA U, his practice lies at the intersection of human and landscape, capturing the idiosincracies of the mundane favoring a slower and more tactile process of analog photography.
