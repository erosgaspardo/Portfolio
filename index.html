(b. 1997) is a photographer and visual artist based in Italy. After taking a BA in Preservation of Cultural Heritage at Ca’ Foscari University of Venice he is currently undertaking the MA in Photography at ISIA U. Mixing digital and analog photography his main focus lies at the intersection of human and landscape, capturing the idiosincracies of the mundane.
